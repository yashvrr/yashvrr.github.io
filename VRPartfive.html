Virtual Reality is the future of education




VR can help with Social Anxiety

People with social anxiety can learn to get more social in VR, by going into tutorial groups that happen in different kinds of apps. You can talk to other students in different classrooms or in different schools (adobe). People in VR teach other how to do a certain task, or problem solve together on a puzzle. As you use VR more, you will become more comfortable with the controls and interactions, and you will develop the skills and confidence you need to fully immerse yourself in virtual environments.
