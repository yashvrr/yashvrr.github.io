Introduction



Biology is the study of living things, including plants, animals, and microbes.
The teacher tells us to turn to a page in a big book so that we can read paragraphs,
illustrations, and graphs that clearly explain plants.
But how would we know how it will seem in three aspects?


How could we learn about the environment needed for survival?
through experiencing it with our physical eyes. Virtual reality is the next step in education;
it is more immersive, interesting, and, very soon, cost-efficient.
